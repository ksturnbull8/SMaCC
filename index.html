About SMaCC

One condition, many specialties — one shared standard

Maternal critical illness rarely fits within a single department. SMaCC brings together the clinicians who manage it — obstetric anaesthesia, maternal medicine, critical care and midwifery — to align practice and share learning across Scottish units.

The network is open to any multidisciplinary professional with an interest in maternal critical care, at all career stages.
