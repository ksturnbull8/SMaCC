About the Conference

The 7th Scottish Maternal Critical Care Symposium

SMaCC '26 is the seventh annual Scottish Maternal Critical Care Symposium, bringing together professionals from across anaesthesia, obstetrics, midwifery, critical care and maternal medicine for a full day of education, updates and shared learning.

The symposium provides education and updates on important topics in maternal critical care, and invites professionals from across the multidisciplinary team to share their experiences, audit, research and quality improvement work in this field.

Registration is open now via BookCPD. The full programme is available to download below.

Abstract Competition

Submit Your Abstract

We invite trainees and consultants from across Scotland to submit abstracts for the SMaCC '26 abstract competition. Submissions are welcome from all disciplines involved in maternal critical care — anaesthesia, obstetrics, midwifery, critical care, and maternal medicine.

Abstracts may cover original research, audit, quality improvement projects or case reports. Successful candidates will be invited to present at the symposium.

Please read the competition guidelines carefully before completing the submission form.
